Ages 6–7 — Learning God’s Mercy and Forgiveness

Week 1 Preview – St. Thérèse of Lisieux

Meet St. Thérèse of Lisieux, our Patron Saint! Known as the Little Flower, she showed the world how to follow Jesus in small, loving ways. Her message was simple: do little things with great love.

Lightbearers Badge:

🕯️ Glowing flame (representing mercy and light)

Spiritual Focus:

  • Understanding right and wrong
  • Knowing how to say sorry and forgive others
  • Trusting in God’s never-ending love

Activity:

Color in your Lightbearers badge and write one kind thing you did this week to share God’s light.

Memory Verse:

“Let the children come to me… for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these.” – Matthew 19:14
