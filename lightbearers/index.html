St. Thérèse of Lisieux – Our Lightbearer Guide

St. Thérèse was a joyful young girl who wanted to bring light into the world by loving God in small ways. She reminds us that every little act of love matters! As our Lightbearer guide, she shows us how to share God's love with everyone through her "Little Way."
